Class-B Autoclave
Advanced Sterilisation Protocol

We treat clinical safety with zero compromise. Our advanced 4-step sterilisation system is designed to deliver maximum protection for every patient.

Step 1: Ultrasonic Debris Dislodgment

Instruments undergo cavitation in an ultrasonic cleaner. High-frequency sound waves dislodge debris from crevices that manual scrubbing cannot reach.

Step 2: Medical-Grade Chemical Disinfection

Instruments are submerged in advanced, concentrated medical-grade clinical disinfectant solution, targeting bacteria, viruses, and fungi to chemically sanitise instruments prior to any packaging.

Step 3: Hermetic Sealed Pouching

Cleaned and sanitised tools are individually dried and placed inside heavy-duty autoclave sterilisation pouches. The pouch is hermetically heat-sealed with internal chemical indicators that change colour, designed to preserve sterility until opened directly in front of you.

Step 4: Class-B Steam Sterilization

The highest safety standard available. Pouched instruments are sterilised using high-pressure vacuum pulses and 134°C saturated steam, achieving clinical-grade sterility in every instrument.

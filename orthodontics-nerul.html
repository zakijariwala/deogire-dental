What the procedure involves

  • 01 Consultation & bite assessment — Digital imaging and clinical examination to assess alignment, bite, and suitability for different treatment options.
  • 02 Treatment planning — A customised plan is developed based on assessment findings. Options may include traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, or clear aligners.
  • 03 Fitting & adjustment — Braces or aligner trays are fitted and periodically adjusted throughout the treatment period to guide teeth into alignment.
  • 04 Retention — A retainer is provided at the end of active treatment to maintain the corrected position.
